## [Modernizing Artifact Storage at Uber](https://www.uber.com/us/en/blog/modernizing-artifact-storage/) 👨‍💼

Uber's artifact storage platform ran across two self-managed data center clusters — five nodes each, replication factor 3, local disk, GeoDNS routing — until hardware failure risk and scaling friction pushed a migration to a SaaS platform with cloud-native blob storage and built-in regional HA. The migration exposed an unexpected cost: the SaaS origin was being hit redundantly on every client request. Uber's fix is a purpose-built validation proxy that issues HTTP conditional headers (If-None-Match, If-Modified-Since) against the origin, returning cached bytes when the artifact hasn't changed — a design generic TTL caches cannot provide because artifact immutability, not staleness, is the freshness model. The proxy delivers 99.99% reliability and cut artifact-related egress costs by roughly 90%, with per-download deduplication compounding to a 99%+ reduction in total egress spend. #devops #engineering #cloud #architecture

## [Monitoring reliably at scale](https://medium.com/airbnb-engineering/monitoring-reliably-at-scale-ca6483040930) 👨‍💼

Building an observability stack on top of the infrastructure it monitors creates a circular dependency: when production degrades, visibility into why degrades with it. At Airbnb, observability traffic already exceeds business traffic by orders of magnitude, and Istio's sidecar model added overhead incompatible with high-throughput telemetry paths. The solution breaks coupling at three layers: dedicated Kubernetes clusters isolate observability compute from production service-mesh failures; a purpose-built proxy tier handles telemetry transport independently of Istio; and a meta-monitoring layer backed by a dead man's switch catches silent failures of the monitoring system itself — the one failure mode that goes completely invisible without it. #devops #engineering #architecture #softwareengineering

## [KernelEvolve: How Meta's Ranking Engineer Agent Optimizes AI Infrastructure](https://engineering.fb.com/2026/04/02/developer-tools/kernelevolve-how-metas-ranking-engineer-agent-optimizes-ai-infrastructure/) 🤓

KernelEvolve treats AI kernel optimization as a structured search problem — not one-shot code generation — pairing Monte Carlo tree search and evolutionary strategies with an LLM synthesizer guided by retrieval-augmented hardware-specific documentation to explore hundreds of kernel implementations per optimization run. Applied to Meta's heterogeneous fleet of NVIDIA, AMD, and custom MTIA accelerators, the system improved Andromeda Ads model inference throughput by 60%+ on NVIDIA GPUs and training throughput by 25%+ on MTIA chips, compressing weeks of expert kernel engineering into hours across trillions of daily inference requests. The approach generalizes beyond Ads Ranking to any model on any accelerator, with the full design presented at ISCA 2026. #ai #engineering #architecture #softwareengineering
