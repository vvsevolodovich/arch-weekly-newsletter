## [From Data Residency to Digital Sovereignty: Architectural Patterns for Cloud Native Platforms](https://www.cncf.io/blog/2026/06/16/from-data-residency-to-digital-sovereignty-architectural-patterns-for-cloud-native-platforms/) 👨‍💼

Digital sovereignty has stopped being a legal footnote and become a platform engineering requirement, with the EU Data Act, NIS-2, DORA, and the UK's rolling Data Use and Access Act all dictating where workloads can run and who can touch them. The pattern platforms use to pass 2026 audits: one tenant cluster per jurisdiction or regulated workload, each with its own control plane declared in Git and reconciled via Argo CD or Flux, with Kyverno or Gatekeeper enforcing policy at the admission-controller layer before a resource ever lands. KubeVirt handles VM workloads on bare metal where containers can't go, Cilium isolates network traffic per tenant, and SPIFFE/SPIRE issues workload identity so trust never crosses jurisdictional lines. It's a concrete blueprint for turning compliance language into cluster topology rather than a paperwork exercise. #cloud #architecture #devops #engineering

## [Improving Arm64 Support in CNCF Projects with OCI Credits](https://www.cncf.io/blog/2026/06/15/improving-arm64-support-in-cncf-projects-with-oci-credits/) 👨‍💼

Oracle Cloud's $3M donation of Ampere Arm64 compute is paying off in CI throughput: builds that used to take hours — or simply time out inside a 6-hour window — when emulated under QEMU now finish natively in 3-5 minutes. Demand backs that up, with usage on the donated capacity jumping from 389K minutes in March to 1.52M minutes by the end of May as more CNCF projects move off emulation. The underlying hardware is no joke either — 160-core dual-socket Ampere Altra bare-metal nodes from Equinix, with individual project runners getting up to 256GB RAM and 80 cores — a reminder that with over 50% of new AWS instances and a third of new Azure instances now on Arm64, native multi-arch CI is no longer optional infrastructure. #devops #cloud #engineering #softwareengineering
