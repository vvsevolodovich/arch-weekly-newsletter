Adding a CPU RAM warm tier cuts time-to-first-token by 40% and end-to-end latency by 30% over pure Lustre offloading; a distributed PVC Evictor handles LRU garbage collection at one replica per 72 TB of capacity. #ai #cloud #architecture #distributed
